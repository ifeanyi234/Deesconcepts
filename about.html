INTRODUCTION

DEES CONCEPTS is a conglomerate of many unique services. Registered in 2019 and distinctively categorized but also put together through a strong structure of management to meet the demand of many business challenges and transactions.

We wish to introduce DEES CONCEPTS as an entity which takes care of all travel and tour needs such as; visa assistance, flight ticketing reservation and hotel bookings, cargo handling, foreign admission processing of its esteemed clients at global level.

Since inception in 2019, DEES CONCEPTS have been venturing into new geographical areas and delivering best services to clients. We offer the complete range of travel related services, superior knowledge, efficient planning and ability to anticipate and resolve potential problems along the way are the reasons behind our success.

At DEES CONCEPTS, traveler safety and security our first priority and the most important aspect of travel. Due to ever changing global space and challenges attached to it. We keep a watchful eye out on our travelers throughout the duration of their journey. We are able to accomplish this through real time traveler tracking based on a single technology platform to ensure accuracy, in addition, we can send messages to mobile devices, alerting travelers of changes to their travel itinerary or issues that ,may arise on their route.

OUR TRAVEL RISK MANAGEMENT INCLUDES:

  • DEES Inform: Travel-Related news update.
  • Travelers tracking reports in real time.
  • DEES Alert: Travel alerts on significant or escalating events.
  • DEES 24 emergency after hours service, available round the clock all year round.
  • Operational support to re-book flights that have been rescheduled or assist with accommodation and ground support.
